NIELIT Imphal Highlights

NIELIT Imphal, a centre of the National Institute of Electronics & Information Technology, operates under the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology, Government of India. Located in the capital city of Manipur, NIELIT Imphal plays a vital role in promoting skill development and IT education in the northeastern region. The institute offers a wide range of courses in electronics, computer science, and information technology, catering to students, professionals, and government employees alike. With modern infrastructure, dedicated faculty, and industry-aligned programs, NIELIT Imphal is committed to empowering youth with technical skills, fostering digital literacy, and contributing to the Digital India mission.

  Why choose NIELIT ?

  NIELIT is a premier institution under the Government   of India, known for delivering quality education and   skill development in the fields of IT, electronics, and   emerging technologies. With a strong presence across   India, it ensures affordable, industry-relevant training and   certifications. NIELIT's nationwide network, flexible   courses, and government-recognized programs make it   a trusted choice   for students and professionals aiming   to build a successful career in the digital era.
